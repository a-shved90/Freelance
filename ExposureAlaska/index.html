Join us in Our 15th Year for great sea kayaking, backpacking, ice climbing, glacier hiking, river rafting and more.

The Best Alaska adventure company you never heard of. We do very little advertising, so you've never read about us in a glossy magazine. Yet our family of professional Guides and adventurous Guests has grown every year. Check out our new Facebook Timeline for a little more on our history.

Our trips offer the smallest group size in the adventure tour business with no more than 6 guests per group. Our maximum of 6 is the minimum for many companies, and we all use the same type of vans! Where would you rather be?

All Inclusive. You have a lot of choices in planning your vacation. Remember that when you join a scheduled Exposure Alaska tour just about everything is included. All the fun stuff. All the technical equipment. All the transportation and accommodation. All the meals. If Ice climbing is on the itinerary, it's included! Rafting? Yep. Dinner out? That too. The only options you might want to add are flight seeing and a beer or two.

Less time in the van. Our trips help you see some of the diversity of Alaska, without spending most of your vacation sitting in a van. We'll get you out there to trek, climb, paddle and explore. Real Alaska backpacking. Expedition sea kayaking.

Leave the tourists behind. We offer more than just day trips. Real sea kayaking where you wake up on a beach overlooking a glacier, not in a campground. Backpack out past where the day hikers dare to go. Our tours aren't for everyone. But they might be for you. See About Us for more on the Exposure difference.
