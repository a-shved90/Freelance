Wow Cow

Retail design

Brief

Come up with a brand and design the interior for a new frozen yoghurt retailer.

Solution

We created something that was modern and fun – just like the product that was being served. We made sure that we maximised the layout of the relatively small space and used every available surface – even the ceiling. The finished product was truly something to be proud of and clearly put Wow Cow on the map.

Results

After operating for only 6 months Wow Cow opened up another store. We think that says it all…
