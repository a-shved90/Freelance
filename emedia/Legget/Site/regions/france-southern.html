Southern France

An area filled with glamour, natural beauty, Mediterranean coastline and some of the most glamorous resorts in the world. It is easy to see why Southern France continues to be perpetually popular with International purchasers.

The Regions of Southern France

  • Provence Alpes Côte d’Azur
  • Languedoc Roussillon

We have included Provence Alpes Côte d'Azur and Languedoc Roussillon in our definition of Southern France. Between the two they cover some of the most exclusive and prestigious property hotspots in France, as well as some of the most rural and rugged locations.

From the glitz of the Cannes film festival, the delicate beauty of Provence and the extraordinary, unspoilt beauty of Languedoc Roussillon. The area boasts artists, film stars and royalty amongst its many fans with some of the finest sporting, cultural and artistic events taking place here.

With either coastline, dramatic mountainous interiors, or both, dominating much of this region, its outstanding beauty makes it a special place.

Couple this with some of France's best vineyards, beautiful iconic towns and villages and a special kind of light that drives artists wild, this is a stunning region which will continue to be incredibly popular with international property purchasers.
