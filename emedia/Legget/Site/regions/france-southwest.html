South West France

The South West of France has been popular with visitors for decades and it is easy to see why. From art, history, food, wine, beaches, mountains, wild countryside and urban living South West France really does have it all.

The Regions of South West France

France is made up of 22 regions. Each region is made up of départements - there are 96 of those in total - and each département is identified with a number. We have included the regions listed below in our definition of Central France;

  • Poitou-Charentes
  • Aquitaine
  • Midi-Pyrénées

Between them, these three regions cover almost every type of landscape and lifestyle you can imagine.

From the drama of the Atlantic coastline to the winter sports of the Pyrénées mountains; the world famous wines of Saint Emilion and Bordeaux to the gastronomic wonderlands of the Perigord and Gascony; the calm tranquillity of the stunning rolling countryside to the beautiful, vibrant cities of Bordeaux, La Rochelle and Biarritz.

This area of France is really special with something to suit almost every taste. Couple that with the famous 'warm welcome' offered to visitors by the hospitable local people, it is easy to see why this region is perennially popular with international property purchasers.
