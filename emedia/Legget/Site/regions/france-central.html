Central France

Central France is what many of us think of when we imagine 'getting away from it all'. Beautiful peaceful countryside, lovely old farmhouses, open space, and nature. The area has become increasingly popular with international purchasers as modern life has become more fast paced, but there is plenty of space to go round and the area still retains its magical tranquillity.

The Regions of Central France

France is made up of 22 regions. Each region is made up of départements - there are 96 of those in total - and each département is identified with a number. We have included the regions listed below in our definition of Central France;

  • Centre
  • Limousin
  • Burgundy
  • Auvergne

This is a huge area encompassing many different landscapes and lifestyles from dramatic mountainous dormant volcanoes, to rolling hills bedecked with vineyards; peaceful river valleys to fairytale French chateaux.

Each of the regions is very different in character, but they all have beautiful countryside, lovely farm properties and plenty of open natural space; at their core. Whether as a bolt-hole to 'get away from it all', or a permanent move to 'the good life', it is no wonder that the area is being discovered as a tonic to modern living by international purchasers.
