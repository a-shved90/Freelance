Discover

the Rhône Alpes

We’re not sure the French Alps need an introduction. They boast the best skiing, most beautiful lakes and mountains and the finest scenery in Europe. Made up of eight very different départements they offer year round entertainment and are a playground for every type of outdoor activity you could wish for.

Bounded by Switzerland and Italy the French Alps belong to one of the greatest mountain ranges in Europe. Mont Blanc, the highest mountain in Europe, caps the range and spans the French-Italian border.

This is a region that offers almost every winter & summer sport and has hosted three Winter Olympics. It’s home to the most renowned ski resorts in the world while to the south it takes on a more Mediterranean flavour. Its capital, Lyon, is known as the gastronomic heart of France.
