Western & Northern France

The North and the North West of France have always been a popular holiday home destination for UK buyers seeking easy access, excellent value for money and beautiful scenery. Steeped in history, this part of France continues to be extremely popular with international purchasers and it is easy to see why.

The Regions of Central France

France is made up of 22 regions. Each region is made up of départements - there are 96 of those in total - and each département is identified with a number. We have included the regions listed below in our definition of Central France;

  • Brittany
  • Lower Normandy
  • Upper Normandy
  • Nord Pas de Calais
  • Picardy
  • Paris & Ile de France
  • Pays de la Loire

This is a huge area encompassing many different landscapes and lifestyles from dramatic mountainous dormant volcanoes, to rolling hills bedecked with vineyards; peaceful river valleys to fairytale French chateaux.

The area has always been popular with the British; with Calais opening the door to ten million visitors a year, all enjoying the easy hop across The Channel to make the most of what this part of France has to offer. From dramatic history, to world class wines; central Parisian chic to forgotten coastal villages; the stunning forests and chateaux of the Loire to the sun and sand of the beaches; this part of France has got something for everyone, and all within easy driving distance of the UK.
