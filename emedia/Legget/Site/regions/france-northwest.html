Western & Northern France

Western & Northern France has always been a popular holiday home destination for UK buyers seeking easy access, excellent value for money and beautiful scenery. Steeped in history, this part of France continues to be extremely popular with international purchasers and it is easy to see why.

The Regions of Western and Northern France

We have included Brittany, Pays de la Loire, Lower Normandy, Upper Normandy, Nord Pas de Calais, Picardy and Paris & Ile de France in our definition of Western and Northern France.

  • Brittany
  • Lower Normandy
  • Upper Normandy
  • Nord Pas de Calais
  • Picardy
  • Paris & Ile de France
  • Pays de la Loire

From the famous chalk cliffs, sweeping sandy beaches and craggy fishing coves to the sophistication and iconic glamour of central Paris, this part of France covers a broad range of landscapes and lifestyles.

The area has always been popular with the British; with Calais opening the door to ten million visitors a year, all enjoying the easy hop across The Channel to make the most of what this part of France has to offer. From dramatic history, to world class wines; central Parisian chic to forgotten coastal villages; the stunning forests and chateaux of the Loire to the sun and sand of the beaches; this part of France has got something for everyone, and all within easy driving distance of the UK.
