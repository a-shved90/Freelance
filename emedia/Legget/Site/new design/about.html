Why use Us?

We pride ourselves on being a family business that offers a friendly and professional service that is far removed from that offered by a franchise or large chain. Perhaps that's why we've been voted "Best Estate Agency in France" for two years running.

If you are looking to buy a property

We have a huge portfolio of properties for sale right across France. From ramshackle barns to convert up to the finest chateaux, villas and ski property we have something to suit every budget.

Our local agents live and work in their area and can offer expert advice on both the regional property market and the best villages, schools, restaurants and local facilities.

Our bi-lingual contracts team will be by your side throughout the purchase process. We even employ our own in-house notaire to ensure each sale progresses as smoothly as possible.

We have been voted “Best Estate Agency in France” for two years running now – we are members of the FNAIM with a carte professionnelle & full professional indemnity insurance and have two decades of experience behind us. We offer local knowledge you can trust.

If you are looking to sell a property

We reach more international buyers than any other agency in France. If you want to expose your property to both the local and global market then you should instruct us. Appoint Leggett Immobilier to sell your property & it will immediately be put in front of 5.4m overseas buyers every month.

Your property will be listed on our website (over 2.4m visitors last year) and we can create a “virtual tour” of your property free of charge so that international buyers get the full picture.

We will also promote your property through a selection of international portals in Europe, Asia and the USA including a guaranteed listing on Rightmove International.

Leggett has an exclusive arrangement with Hamptons International who market our properties through their local offices, wealth management department and dedicated website.

Our marketing budget of €1m pa allows us to exhibit properties at major exhibitions around the world as well as advertise in publications such as the FT and Country Life International.

Our team speak English, French, Dutch, Chinese, Russian and a host of other languages – we will be by your side to ensure that the sale passes as smoothly as possible.
