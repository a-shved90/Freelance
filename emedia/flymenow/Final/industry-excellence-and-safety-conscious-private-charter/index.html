Industry Excellence

FlyMeNow is committed to excellence and as such is a member of the following leading aviation bodies.

BACA - The Baltic Air Charter Association (BACA) represents the interests of commercial aviation companies particularly in the air charter industry. Its principal objective is to promote integrity and ethical business practices. This ensures everyone can have confidence in a member of the BACA.

EBAA - European Business Aviation Association (EBAA) aims to promote excellence and professionalism amongst its members to enable them to deliver best-in-class safety and operational efficiency.

Wyvern: As certified and pledged Wyvern brokers, we are committed to safety and fair business practice throughout our work. The Wyvern system we use allows us to generate real-time safety reports for clients on the operator, aircraft and crew of any particular Wyvern operated flight.

BHAB - The British Helicopter Advisory Board (BHAB) is the civil helicopter industry's trade association. Its main objective is to promote the use of helicopters in the UK and to bring to the attention of potential users the advantages of using or owning a helicopter. It is also concerned that helicopter operations are conducted safely and responsibly and that proper attention is paid to environmental issues.

British Ambulance Association (BAA) is the largest representative body for the Private Ambulance sector covering the entire United Kingdom and beyond. FlyMeNow are proud to be members with vast experience undertaking Private Air Ambulance and Medical Repatriation flights.
